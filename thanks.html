Congratulations!! You are now ready to draft your IDP!

Ask yourself these questions for a SMART IDP :

  • 1. What is your career vision (2-3 years) and aspirations? What roles would you like to do next?

  • 2. What are your strengths and focus areas of improvement?

  • 3. What capabilities/ skills do you want to develop?

  • 4. What formats of development would you want to explore? On the job, coaching/ mentoring or any formal programs?

  • 5. What are constraints that may emerge in the next few months?
